MasterMind is a simple game. You get 10 tries to guess the secret 4 color comibnation randomly selected by the computer. Just click/toggle the circles into the color combination you want to guess then click the submit button to the right. The result of the guess will appear where the submit button was.

A black dot indicates a correct color at the correct position. A white dot indicates a correct color at the wrong position. Use this info to guide you on your next guess. Four black dots is a win.
